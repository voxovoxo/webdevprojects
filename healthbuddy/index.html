Our App is designed to help you MONITOR and improve your MENTAL well-being by providing a user-friendly interface to TRACK your emotions, moods, and activities.

At Mental Health Tracker, we understand that maintaining good mental health is essential for overall well-being.

By keeping a record of your mental health journey, you can gain valuable insights into your emotional patterns and make positive changes in your life.

Features
Daily Mood Tracker

Keep track of your daily moods using our intuitive mood tracker. Simply select the emotion that best represents your feelings for the day, and you'll be able to visualize your mood trends over time.

Emotion Journal

Maintain an emotion journal where you can write about your feelings, thoughts, and experiences. Reflect on your day, celebrate the positive moments, and process challenging emotions in a safe and private space.

Activity Log

Record your daily activities and routines to identify potential triggers or patterns that may impact your mental well-being. Understanding how your activities affect your mood can empower you to make informed lifestyle choices.

Reminders and Goal Setting

Set reminders to check in with yourself regularly and stay consistent in tracking your emotions. Additionally, establish mental health goals and monitor your progress towards achieving them.

Community Support

Connect with a community of like-minded individuals who are also on a journey to improve their mental health. Share experiences, offer support, and learn from others in a supportive and non-judgmental environment.

Insightful Resources

Access a library of articles, tips, and resources curated by mental health experts. Learn coping strategies, mindfulness techniques, and other valuable insights to enhance your mental well-being.

How to Get Started:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up for a free account to get started on your mental health journey. We only require a valid email address to ensure your data remains secure.
  2. Complete Initial Assessment: To provide you with the best experience, we'll ask you a few questions to understand your current emotional state and tailor the platform to your needs.
  3. Start Tracking: Begin logging your daily moods, emotions, and activities to build a comprehensive picture of your mental health.
  4. Set Goals: Define your mental health goals and let the platform assist you in achieving them.
  5. Connect with the Community: Join our supportive community and participate in discussions to gain insights and share experiences.
